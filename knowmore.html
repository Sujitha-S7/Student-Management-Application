About Us

Welcome to Arminsjova Institute of Engineering and Technology, where innovation and excellence in engineering education take center stage. Our institution is dedicated to nurturing the brightest minds in the field of engineering. With a dynamic and supportive learning environment, cutting-edge facilities, and a world-class faculty, we provide the ideal platform for students to explore, learn, and realize their full potential. At Arminsjova, we believe in pushing the boundaries of knowledge and fostering creativity to shape the engineers of tomorrow. Join us on this exciting journey towards excellence and discovery, as we work together to build a brighter future through technology and innovation.

  • Master of Science in Civil Engineering

Faculty

Our esteemed faculty members, comprising a team of dedicated professionals, are experts in their respective fields. With a commitment to imparting knowledge, nurturing the talents of our students, and promoting academic excellence, they form the backbone of our institution. Our faculty includes:

  • 60 Professors with extensive experience and knowledge in various engineering disciplines.
  • 50 Associate Professors who contribute to teaching, research, and mentorship.
  • 40+ Technicians and lab instructors supporting hands-on learning and research activities.
  • 20+ Researchers actively engaged in groundbreaking research and innovation.

Our faculty members are not only educators but also mentors and role models who inspire our students to excel in their chosen fields and contribute to the world of engineering and technology.
